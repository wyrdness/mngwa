## Source Quality Assessment

### Tier 1: Highest Quality
- William Hitchens' published articles and official reports
- Colonial administrative records (contemporary documentation)
- Academic zoological works on African felids
- Peer-reviewed scientific papers on cat biology

### Tier 2: Good Quality
- Heuvelmans' cryptozoological analysis
- Shuker's comprehensive mystery cat research
- Professional hunter memoirs (experienced observers)
- Historical newspaper accounts (contemporary but popular press)

### Tier 3: Moderate Quality
- General cryptozoology encyclopedias
- Secondary analysis by modern researchers
- Documentary presentations
- Online databases (variable quality)

### Tier 4: Lower Quality (Use with Caution)
- Unverified online reports
- Social media accounts
- Sensationalized media coverage
- Sources without clear provenance
